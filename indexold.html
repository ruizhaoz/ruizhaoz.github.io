SelfD: Self-Learning Large-Scale Driving Policies From the Web
Jimuyang Zhang, Ruizhao Zhu, Eshed Ohn-Bar
Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2022
arxiv  /  video

SelfD is a new semi-supervised framework learning scalable driving by utilizing large amounts of online monocular images. SelfD demonstrates consistent improvements (by up to 24%) in driving performance evaluation on nuScenes, Argoverse, Waymo, and CARLA.
